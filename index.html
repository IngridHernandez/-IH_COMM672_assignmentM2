Tamales

Rice

When serving tamales for dinner, choosing complementary side dishes can guarantee an elevated meal. Mexican rice, with its rich flavors and vibrant colors, pairs beautifully with tamales.

Beans

Refried beans, with their creamy texture and savory flavor, provide a hearty complement to tamales. Crafting a homemade recipe is simple and allows for control over ingredients, making it easy to create vegetarian options.
